i.Profile

The best design leadership is invisible — not because design is absent, but because it’s so embedded in how a business thinks that it becomes indistinguishable from strategy.

Over 13 years I’ve built and led globally distributed design teams, shaped product strategy at C-suite level, and driven outcomes across millions of users in complex B2C ecosystems. At Just Eat Takeaway I’ve grown from Senior Designer to Design Leadership — scaling a discovery framework adopted by a 150+ person org, launching a £4M+ product, and designing AI-integrated experiences that measurably shift behaviour.
