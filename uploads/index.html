No accountant jargon.
Klaio talks to you in plain English.

Every existing reconciliation tool posts data to Xero. None of them explain what that data means in language a non-accountant can understand. Klaio does both — automatically, for every payout, from day one.

01

Klaio reads your payout

Every time Shopify issues a payout, Klaio pulls the full breakdown — gross sales, fees, refunds, chargebacks — to individual order level.

02

It checks every line against Xero

Each component is verified against your Xero bank feed and expense accounts. Missing entries are flagged. Duplicates are caught. The numbers are made to agree.

03

Then it writes you a plain-English summary

Not a journal entry. Not a reconciliation report. A sentence — or two — that explains what happened in your payout in language anyone can understand. No accounting knowledge needed.

04

One click to fix anything that needs fixing

If Klaio finds a missing fee, a refund crossing VAT periods, or a chargeback not in Xero — it prepares the fix and posts it with one click. You confirm. It posts. Done.
